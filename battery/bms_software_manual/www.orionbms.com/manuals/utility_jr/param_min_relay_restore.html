Minimum Relay Restore Time

Units: [Seconds]

The minimum amount of time before either the Discharge Enable, Charger Safety or Charge Enable relays can be re-enabled after the relay amperage restore conditions are met (if set). This can be used to help prevent oscillation of the relay.

Note: This value is shared between all three relay outputs.
